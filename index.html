About The Grand Ballroom

Established in 2010, The Grand Ballroom has been a premier venue for countless memorable occasions. Our elegant hall can comfortably accommodate up to 500 guests, making it perfect for large gatherings or intimate affairs. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and a seamless experience for every client. We offer flexible seating arrangements and decor options. Our professional event planning team is here to assist you every step of the way, from the initial planning stages to the execution of your vision. At The Grand Ballroom, we’re not just providing a space—we’re creating moments.
