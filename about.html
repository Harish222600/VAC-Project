Our Story of Hosting Memorable Events

Since 2010

A premier venue for academic, cultural, and corporate events

Established in 2010, the SAEC Auditorium has been a cornerstone of excellence in hosting events at SA Engineering College. From academic seminars and workshops to cultural festivals and corporate meetings, our auditorium has been the preferred choice for a wide range of events.

Our mission is to provide a world-class venue equipped with modern facilities and exceptional service. Over the years, we've hosted over 1,000 events, each one contributing to our legacy of excellence.
